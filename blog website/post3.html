If love ever decides to knock on my door again…

A Journey of Hope, Healing, and Second Chances


Vina's Memories
1 min read | Apr 4,2024

If love should grace my doorstep again, I long for a patient soul who waits expectantly, bearing not empty hands but a constellation of stars to rival my own brilliance. May they be a navigator of our shared journey, eschewing ambiguity for a clear path forward, inscribing my name in the records of our destiny.

If love circles back into my life, let it be with a steadfast companion who sees beyond uncertainty, embracing our future with unwavering certainty. May they sketch my name into the fabric of their tomorrows, weaving our stories together with purpose.

If love pays me another visit, let it be with someone who understands that storms may come, yet chooses to remain steadfast. May they speak not in whispers but in deeds, their actions a testament to their unwavering commitment.

And if love once more knocks upon my door, may I find the courage to welcome it with open arms.
